Whispers of the Night

by A. Dreamer

The moonlight dances on the sea,
A silver song of mystery.
The stars above in gentle flight,
Paint whispers through the velvet night.

The breeze hums soft, the waves reply,
Their voices blend in lullaby.
The world grows still, the heart takes flight,
Lost in dreams beneath the light.
